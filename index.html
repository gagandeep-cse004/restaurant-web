25 Years of
Excellence

Our Culinary Legacy

Founded in 1999, ParthEatery Dayal has been a beacon of culinary excellence in the heart of the city. Our journey began with a simple dream: to create an extraordinary dining experience that celebrates the finest ingredients and impeccable craftsmanship.

Today, we continue to honor that legacy, blending traditional techniques with innovative approaches to create dishes that delight and inspire. Every plate tells a story, and every meal is a celebration.
